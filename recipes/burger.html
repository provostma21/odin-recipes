Ingredients

  • Hamburger buns
  • 2 lbs Ground Beef
  • 1/4 lb Ground Pork
  • 8 oz Baby Portobella Mushrooms
  • 1 Sweet Onion
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/4 lb Swiss Cheese
  • 1 Tbs Butter
  • Worchester Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Marsala Wine

Steps

  1. Dice up the garlic.
  2. Cut the mushrooms and onion up into slices.
  3. Place the ground beef and ground pork into a bowl.
  4. Add the egg and mix the beef, pork, egg, and some salt and pepper.
  5. Make some patties out of the ground meat.
  6. Bring up a sautee pan to medium heat and add butter.
  7. Sautee the sliced onion until it starts to get transparent.
  8. Add the mushrooms, then add some marsala after a few minutes.
  9. Once the onions are turning a golden brown, add the garlic, salt, and pepper.
  10. Take the mushroom and onions off heat 1 minute after adding the garlic.
  11. Add garlic powder, salt, pepper, and worchester sauce onto the burger patties.
  12. Now add the patties onto the grill.
  13. Once you flip the patties, make sure to add the cheese.
  14. When the patties are down: Place them on the buns and add your mushroom and onions.
  15. Enjoy!
