Fried Rice

Description

Ever wonder how to make the same savory fried rice you get from Japanese Habachi- style restaurants? Well look no further! Here, we will list everything you need to make the best habachi-style fried rice. Flat-top grill not included, though not absolutely necessary either.

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of White or Brown rice of your choice. We used jasmine here.
  • 1/2 lb Carrots
  • 4 Shoots of Scallions
  • 1 Cup of Peas
  • 2 Sweet Onions
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup of Sake
  • 1/2 Cup of Soy Sauce
  • 5 Tbs of Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Make up the 3 Cups of Rice the day before the rest for stickier rice.
  2. Dice up the carrots, scallions, onions, and garlic and keep separate.
  3. Put 1 Tbs into a medium sautee pan at medium heat.
  4. Throw in the carrots first, followed by the onions and peas.
  5. When onions begin to caramalize, add in the scallions.
  6. Add a smidge of salt and pepper.
  7. Once the onions are beginning to brown: add the garlic.
  8. Keep the pan on for another 30-60 seconds and then remove from heat.
  9. Now, bring out a large frying pan or wok and raise to medium-high heat.
  10. Add 1 Tbs of butter, followed by the rice from before.
  11. After rice is up to heat, add your sauteed veggies.
  12. Add your 2 eggs.
  13. Now add your 1/2 Cup of Sake and 1/2 Cup of Soy Sauce.
  14. Mix well and add salt/pepper to taste.
  15. If you need it, add more soy sauce or sake.
  16. Finally, enjoy!
