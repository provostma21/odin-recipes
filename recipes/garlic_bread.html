Ingredients

  • 2 Loaves of Italian Bread
  • 4 Sticks of Lightly Salted Butter
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Mince garlic, melt butter, and cut the bread into slices (Not all the way through the bottom of the loaf, though).
  2. Mix some in the garlic, parsley, and oregano into the butter.
  3. Pour melted butter onto loaves, make sure to get in between the slices.
  4. Wrap loaves up in tin foil and put into oven at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
